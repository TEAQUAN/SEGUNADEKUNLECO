OUR FIRM

Messrs SEGUN ADEKUNLE & COMPANY is a well-established firm of Estate Surveyors and Valuers in Nigeria. The company was registered in 2002, with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with RC. No. LAZ. 131623 and granted a certificate of compliance by the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (ESVARBON), allowing them to practice the profession of Estate Surveying and Valuation Nationwide. Our firm is tax compliant with tax Identification number (TIN): 18885864-0001.

OUR VISION

At SEGUN ADEKUNLE & COMPANY, our vision is to be a leader in the profession of Estate Surveying and Valuation. QUALITY ASSURANCE

At Segun Adekunle & Company, we maintain rigorous quality control processes to ensure that our services meet and exceed industry standards. Our quality assurance program includes:

Regular peer reviews of valuation reports, continuous professional development for all team members, strict adherence to national and international valuation standards, and implementation of a comprehensive quality management system that guides our operations from client engagement through to project completion.

Our commitment to quality has earned us recognition within the industry and the trust of our diverse clientele. We continuously evaluate and improve our processes to deliver exceptional service in every project we undertake.

MR. SEGUN ADEKUNLE

B.Sc., MSC. ANIVS, RSV

He is the principal partner of the firm. A graduate of Estate Management from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife, Osun State and an associate member of the Nigeria Institution of Estate Surveyors & Valuers. He has been in practice for about a decade; he was an associate partner of Messrs Jide Taiwo & Co. before establishing his professional practice in 2002. His areas of specialization are valuation, project management and Real Estate Agency. Aside his varying experience in all aspect of Estate Management he holds a postgraduate Diploma in Banking and Finance from Bayero University, Kano. He has attended several courses and seminars on professional practice.
