Our Services

Professional Valuation Solutions for Your Needs

Comprehensive Valuation Services

We carry out valuations of buildings and Chattels, Crop/Economic trees enumeration, Plant and Machinery, Stock Exchange Quotations, General Accountancy, Insurance and Taxation. This we carry out by the integration of high Professional Standards and Research combined with market forces to arrive at a fair value.

Valuation requires a logical process, which involves a scientific solution to a valuation request. We approach valuation by specifying the physical structures involved, the rights to be valued, the purpose of valuation, type of value sought and the date at which valuation will apply.

The resultant valuation will be presented in a report encompassing the data collected, our observation of findings and opinion of value of the property or interest based on the purpose for which the exercise is been intended.

We have undertaken several valuation jobs on behalf of our clients, banks, corporate organizations, States and Federal Government etc. We have also played advisory roles as independent Valuers to banks on the value of properties mortgaged by their customers.

Building Valuation

Professional valuation of residential, commercial, and industrial buildings for various purposes including sales, mortgages, and insurance.

Crop & Economic Trees

Enumeration and valuation of crops and economic trees for compensation, taxation, or agricultural finance purposes.

Plant & Machinery

Professional valuation of industrial plants, machinery, and equipment for financial reporting, insurance, or sale purposes.

Stock Exchange Quotations

Valuation of securities and stock exchange quotations for investment, financial reporting, or regulatory compliance.

Accountancy Services

General accountancy services including financial statement preparation, bookkeeping, and business advisory.

Insurance & Taxation

Valuation for insurance purposes and tax planning, including asset valuation for estate and property taxes.
